This Site

Curated by David Colarusso on GitGub.

The pages found under wejudicate.gethub.io/incourt "This Site" are a collection of materials the authors have found useful to have on hand in court. You'll find three flavours of content here:

  • good old fashioned links (including deep links) with citation;
  • resources re-formatted for mobile and served from This Site (either in the public domain or used with permission); and
  • interactive aids called guesstimators.

    Since This Site is optimized for mobile browsing, links aren't always depicted by blue underlined text. All pages on This Site, however, have the same light blue navigation bar at the top.

    A list of content sources can be found by clicking on the white and gray information buttons on a page's navigation bar. Prior to August 2014, this site was hosted at DavidColarusso.com.

    Also, special thanks to Flickr user Manu_H's (Emmanuel Huybrechts) for his picture of the John Adams Courthouse. It can be found at the Wikimedia Commons and is used here under a Creative Commons Attribution 2.0 Generic license. Variations on the image are used in this page's Open Graph preview as well as its mobile icon.
