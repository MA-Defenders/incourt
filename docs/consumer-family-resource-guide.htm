Information and Referral for Consumers and Families

December 2012

 

 

 

 

 

 

The Department of Mental Health operates a toll-free Information and Referral line at 800-221-0053. The call center is located within the DMH Office of Training and Consumer Affairs and is staffed by a DMH Information and Referral Specialist. The DMH Information and Referral Monday is for everyone and is available Monday through Friday, 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. for basic information and/or referral sources. All calls are confidential and voice messages are promptly returned.

 

While DMH cannot always provide specific information such as referrals to specific therapists, numerous general referral sources are available to callers, often in their geographic areas. Responses are tailored to the needs of a caller and reflect the Departments commitment to a person-center, recovery-oriented system of care. The following list of categories of some of our available information and resources:
